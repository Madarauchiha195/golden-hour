GOLDEN HOUR MEDICAL COORDINATOR - SBAR HANDOFF TEMPLATES

=== SBAR FORMAT OVERVIEW ===
SBAR (Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation) is the standardized communication framework for all hospital handoff notifications in the Golden Hour system.

=== TEMPLATE: CARDIAC EMERGENCY SBAR ===

SITUATION:
"This is [dispatcher/EMT name] from [unit/station]. We are transporting a [age]-year-old [sex] patient presenting with [chief complaint]. Patient is currently [stable/unstable]. ETA to your facility is [X] minutes. Ambulance [ID] is en route with [ALS/BLS/Critical Care] equipment."

BACKGROUND:
"Patient reports onset of symptoms [duration] ago. Medical history includes [relevant history]. Current medications: [medications]. Known allergies: [allergies]. Patient [has/has not] taken aspirin. [Additional relevant history: prior cardiac events, surgical history, family history of cardiac disease]."

ASSESSMENT:
"Triage category: Cardiology. Severity: [Critical/High/Medium/Low]. Vital signs: HR [X], BP [X/X], RR [X], SpO2 [X]%, Temp [X]. ECG shows [findings if available]. Patient is [alert/confused/unconscious]. Pain level: [X/10]. Risk assessment: Survival probability [X]%, Urgency score [X/10]."

RECOMMENDATION:
"Requesting [cath lab activation / cardiac monitoring / cardiology consult]. Please prepare [specific equipment: crash cart, defibrillator, thrombolytics, cardiac catheterization lab]. Recommend [IV access, cardiac enzymes, serial ECGs, chest X-ray]. Estimated preparation time needed: [X] minutes."

=== TEMPLATE: NEUROLOGICAL EMERGENCY SBAR ===

SITUATION:
"This is [dispatcher/EMT name]. We are transporting a [age]-year-old [sex] with acute [stroke symptoms/seizure/head injury]. Symptom onset at [time]. Last known well at [time]. ETA [X] minutes via Ambulance [ID]."

BACKGROUND:
"Patient history: [hypertension, diabetes, atrial fibrillation, prior stroke/TIA, anticoagulation use]. Current medications: [list, especially anticoagulants]. Allergies: [list]. [Witnessed/unwitnessed] event. [Any interventions already performed]."

ASSESSMENT:
"Triage: Neurology, Severity: [level]. GCS: [X] (E[X]V[X]M[X]). NIHSS estimated: [X if available]. Pupils: [findings]. Focal deficits: [describe]. Vitals: HR [X], BP [X/X], RR [X], SpO2 [X]%, BGL [X]. Survival probability: [X]%, Urgency: [X/10]."

RECOMMENDATION:
"Requesting [stroke team activation / neurosurgery consult / CT scan immediate]. Please prepare [CT scanner, tPA if within window, neurology team, ICU bed]. Time-critical: [within tPA window yes/no]. Recommend [immediate CT head, CTA, labs including coagulation panel]."

=== TEMPLATE: TRAUMA SBAR ===

SITUATION:
"Trauma alert. [Age]-year-old [sex], [mechanism of injury]. Patient is [stable/unstable/critical]. GCS [X]. ETA [X] minutes, Ambulance [ID], [ALS/BLS/Critical] level."

BACKGROUND:
"Mechanism: [details - speed, height, weapon, etc.]. Injuries identified: [list]. Interventions performed: [C-spine immobilization, hemorrhage control, IV access, intubation]. Medical history: [relevant]. Medications: [especially anticoagulants]. Allergies: [list]. Tetanus status: [if known]."

ASSESSMENT:
"Triage: Trauma, Severity: [level]. Primary survey: A-[patent/compromised], B-[RR, SpO2, breath sounds], C-[HR, BP, hemorrhage sites], D-[GCS, pupils], E-[injuries noted, temp]. Estimated blood loss: [volume]. Survival probability: [X]%, Urgency: [X/10]."

RECOMMENDATION:
"Requesting [trauma team activation / OR standby / massive transfusion protocol]. Prepare [trauma bay, blood products, surgical team, CT scanner, chest tube tray]. Specialty needs: [orthopedic, neurosurgery, vascular]. Estimated prep time: [X] minutes."

=== TEMPLATE: RESPIRATORY EMERGENCY SBAR ===

SITUATION:
"Respiratory emergency. [Age]-year-old [sex] with [acute respiratory distress/asthma/COPD/PE]. Current SpO2 [X]% on [room air/O2 at X L/min]. ETA [X] minutes."

BACKGROUND:
"History of [respiratory conditions]. Current medications: [inhalers, steroids, home O2]. Recent illness: [URI, pneumonia]. Allergies: [list]. Smoking history: [pack-years]. Previous intubations: [yes/no]."

ASSESSMENT:
"Triage: Respiratory, Severity: [level]. Work of breathing: [mild/moderate/severe]. Breath sounds: [findings]. SpO2: [X]% on [O2 delivery]. RR: [X], HR: [X], BP: [X/X]. Accessory muscle use: [yes/no]. Able to speak in: [full sentences/phrases/words/unable]. Survival probability: [X]%."

RECOMMENDATION:
"Requesting [respiratory therapy / intubation setup / BiPAP/CPAP / ICU bed]. Prepare [ventilator, bronchodilators, IV steroids, chest X-ray, ABG kit]. If suspected PE: [CT angiography, heparin]. Estimated prep: [X] minutes."

=== TEMPLATE: GENERAL EMERGENCY SBAR ===

SITUATION:
"Emergency transport. [Age]-year-old [sex] presenting with [chief complaint]. Patient is [stable/unstable]. ETA [X] minutes via Ambulance [ID]."

BACKGROUND:
"Onset [duration] ago. Associated symptoms: [list]. Medical history: [relevant conditions]. Surgical history: [relevant]. Medications: [list]. Allergies: [list]. Last oral intake: [time]."

ASSESSMENT:
"Triage: General Emergency, Severity: [level]. Vitals: HR [X], BP [X/X], RR [X], SpO2 [X]%, Temp [X], BGL [X]. Physical exam: [key findings]. Survival probability: [X]%, Urgency: [X/10]."

RECOMMENDATION:
"Requesting [specific consult/service]. Prepare [specific equipment/medications]. Labs recommended: [CBC, BMP, coags, type and screen, etc.]. Imaging: [CT, ultrasound, X-ray]. Estimated prep: [X] minutes."

=== SBAR QUALITY CHECKLIST ===
1. Patient identifiers present (age, sex)
2. Chief complaint clearly stated
3. ETA and transport unit identified
4. Relevant medical history included
5. Current vital signs documented
6. Severity and risk assessment included
7. Specific resource requests made
8. Preparation time estimated
